Nexva uses **Dodo Payments** and **CREAM** as secure payment providers to manage subscriptions, billing cycles, and transactions.

This guide explains how subscriptions work, how payments are processed, and how to manage, upgrade, or cancel your plan.

---

## Payment Providers

Nexva integrates with the following payment providers:

- **Dodo Payments** – Subscription billing, renewals, and invoices
- **CREAM** – Secure payment processing and transaction handling

These providers ensure reliable, encrypted, and compliant payment processing.

---

## Accessing Billing Settings

You can manage your subscription from:

**Avatar → My Account → Billing**

---

## Managing Your Subscription

### View Current Plan

From the Billing page, you can:

- See your active subscription plan
- View plan features and usage limits
- Check your next billing date
- Monitor remaining credits

---

### Upgrade or Change Plan

You can upgrade or change your plan at any time.

**How to upgrade or change plans:**
1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan** or **Change Plan**
3. Select a new plan
4. Complete payment via Dodo Payments or CREAM

Plan changes usually take effect immediately after successful payment.

---

## Billing Cycle & Renewals

### Billing Cycle

- Subscriptions are billed on a **monthly or yearly basis**
- The billing date is based on the day you subscribed
- Billing is handled automatically by the payment provider

---

### Automatic Renewals

- Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing cycle
- Payments are charged using your saved payment method
- You can stop renewals by canceling your subscription before the renewal date

---

## Payment Methods

Payment methods supported depend on your region and provider.

### Supported Methods

- Credit cards
- Debit cards
- Other region-specific payment options (via Dodo Payments or CREAM)

Payment details are processed securely and are not stored directly on Nexva servers.

---

## Billing History & Invoices

### View Billing History

From the Billing section, you can:

- View all past transactions
- Check payment status (successful, pending, failed)
- See transaction dates and amounts

---

### Download Invoices

- Invoices are generated after successful payments
- Download invoices for accounting or records
- Invoice availability depends on the payment provider

---

## Canceling Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time.

### How to Cancel

1. Open **My Account → Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm the cancellation

---

### What Happens After Cancellation

- Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing cycle
- No further charges will be made
- Paid features remain accessible until expiration
- After expiry, your account switches to the **Free** plan

---

## Refund Policy

- Payments are generally **non-refundable**
- Refunds may be considered in exceptional cases
- Refund eligibility depends on usage and provider policies

Contact support if you believe you were charged incorrectly.

---

## Common Payment Issues

### Payment Failed

Possible reasons include:
- Insufficient balance
- Expired card
- Bank or gateway restrictions
- Network or provider issues

**Recommended actions:**
- Retry the payment
- Use a different payment method
- Contact your bank if needed

---

### Payment Successful but Plan Not Updated

If your payment is successful but your plan doesn’t update immediately:

- Refresh the page
- Log out and log back in
- Wait a few minutes for provider confirmation

If the issue persists, contact support with your transaction details.

---

### Duplicate or Unexpected Charges

- Review your billing history
- Ensure only one active subscription exists
- Contact support if discrepancies are found

---

## Security & Privacy

- All payments are processed through secure gateways
- Card data is encrypted and protected
- Nexva does not store full payment credentials
