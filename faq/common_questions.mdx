---

### What is Nexva?

Nexva is an AI platform that allows you to access multiple AI models in one place.
You can chat with AI, generate images, analyze files, use prompts, and run advanced workflows.

---

### Who is Nexva for?

Nexva is designed for:
- Individuals and professionals
- Developers and non-technical users
- Content creators and marketers
- Students and researchers
- Businesses and teams

No technical background is required.

---

### Do I need to install anything?

No.
Nexva works entirely in your web browser. No downloads or installations are required.
