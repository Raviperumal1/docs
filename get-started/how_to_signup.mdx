Signing in to Nexva gives you access to AI chat, image generation, advanced tools, saved conversations, and account settings.

This guide explains **how to sign in step by step**, the available sign-in methods, and how to resolve common login issues.

---

## What You Need Before Signing In

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A stable internet connection
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari)
- An existing Nexva account (email or social login)

If you do not have an account yet, you must sign up first.
