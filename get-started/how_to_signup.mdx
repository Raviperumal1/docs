Creating an account on Nexva gives you access to AI chat, image generation, video generation, advanced tools, saved conversations, and personalized settings.

This guide explains **how to create an account step by step**, the available sign-up methods, and helpful tips for a smooth registration process.

---

## What You Need Before Signing Up

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A stable internet connection
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari)
- A valid email address for verification
- Access to your email inbox

---

![signup](/images/signup.png)

## Step 1: Open the Nexva Website

1. Open your web browser
2. Visit the Nexva homepage
3. Click the **Sign Up** button

---

## Step 2: Choose a Sign-Up Method

You can create an account using one of the following methods:

### Option A: Sign Up with Google

1. Click **Sign Up with Google**
2. Select your Google account
3. Grant the required permissions

Your Nexva's account will be created instantly.

### Option B: Sign Up with Email

1. Enter your **Full Name**
2. Enter your **Email Address**
3. Create a **Password**
4. Agree to the **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy**
5. Click **Create Account**

## Step 3: Verify Your Email Address

1. Check your email inbox
2. Open the verification email from Nexva
3. Click the **Verification Link**

Your account will be activated after verification.

## Step 4: Log In to Your Account

Once verified, you can:

- Log in to Nexva
- Start AI chat and assistance
- Generate images and videos
- Access advanced AI tools
- Manage your account settings

## Tips for a Successful Sign-Up

- Use a valid email address for notifications and account recovery
- Choose a strong and unique password
- Check your spam folder if you don’t see the verification email
- Complete email verification to unlock full features

## Common Issues

### Didn’t Receive the Verification Email?
- Check spam or junk folders
- Ensure the email address is correct
- Request a new verification email if available

### Unable to Sign Up?
- Try using a different browser
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Ensure a stable internet connection

## Getting Started

Once your account is created and verified, log in and start using Nexva’s AI assistance immediately.
