## Profile Information

The **Profile** section displays your basic account details, including:

- Full name
- Email address
- Profile picture (if available)
- Account creation date

You can review and update your profile information at any time.

---

## Billing & Subscription

The **Billing** section helps you manage your subscription and payment details.

### Current Plan

- View your active plan
- Check usage limits and features
- Upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time

### Billing History & Invoices

- View past transactions
- Download invoices and receipts
- Track payment dates and amounts

All billing information is securely handled.

---

## Security Settings

### Change Password

You can update your password to keep your account secure:

1. Open the **Security** or **Password** section
2. Enter your current password
3. Set a new password
4. Save changes

Choose a strong password for better security.

---

## General Settings

The **General Settings** section lets you control core account actions.

### Clear All Chats

- Permanently delete all chat history
- This action cannot be undone
- Useful for privacy or a fresh start

### Delete Account

- Permanently remove your Nexva account
- Deletes all data, chats, and settings
- This action is irreversible

You may be asked to confirm your password before proceeding.
