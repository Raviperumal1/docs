# AI Content Detection

The Content Detection feature of Sider is your one-stop solution for an enriched chatting experience. You can complete nearly all tasks in it. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Content Detection Feature Introduction

- **AI Models:** Choose to chat with GPT-3.5, GPT-4, Claude 3 Haiku, Claude 3 Sonnet, Claude 3 Opus, or Gemini. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
- **Screenshot:** Take a screenshot of any content on any page, so you can chat with the screenshot further. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
- **Upload Files:** Upload a file from your computer to start a conversation with it. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
- **Read this page:** Summarize or chat with the current webpage or YouTube video. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
- **Prompts:** Built-in frequently used prompts, saving you time inputting prompts manually. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
- **Mention Bot:** Click to mention one or multiple AI bots to answer the same question so you can compare the answers conveniently. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}
- **Tools:** Enable advanced tools, including Web Access, Painter, or Advanced Data Analysis, to super-charge your AI conversation. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
- **Copy:** Click to copy the response. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
- **Quote:** Click to quote the response and then ask further questions based on it. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}
- **Regenerate response:** Click to regenerate response. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}
- **Ask other AI Model:** Click to get response from other AI models or from the web. :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}
- **History:** View your chatting history. :contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}
- **New Content Detection:** Click to start a new chat. :contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}
