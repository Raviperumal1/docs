# Audio Transcription (Mic to Text)

Audio Transcription allows you to convert spoken words into written text by recording audio directly from your microphone. With a single click, you can start recording, speak naturally, and stop the recording to instantly get transcribed text.

---

## How to Record Audio

1. Click the **üé§ Microphone** button
2. Your microphone will activate and start recording
3. Speak clearly into the microphone
4. Click the **‚èπ Stop Recording** button
5. Your speech will be automatically converted into text

---

## What You Can Do

- Convert live speech into text
- Transcribe meetings, interviews, or notes
- Dictate content instead of typing
- Use transcribed text for further analysis
- Edit or copy the generated text instantly

---

## Supported Features

- Real-time microphone recording
- Automatic speech-to-text conversion
- Multi-language speech recognition *(if enabled)*
- Editable transcription output
- Fast and lightweight processing

---

## Best Practices for Accurate Transcription

- Use a clear microphone or headset
- Speak at a normal, steady pace
- Reduce background noise
- Avoid overlapping voices
- Pause briefly between sentences

---

## Limitations

- Accuracy depends on audio quality
- Strong background noise may reduce accuracy
- Heavy accents may require clearer pronunciation
- Very long recordings may take extra processing time

---

## Privacy & Security

- Audio is processed only for transcription
- No recordings are stored unless explicitly saved
- Transcription data is handled securely

---

## Example Use Cases

- Voice notes and journaling
- Content creation and blogging
- Interview and meeting transcription
- Accessibility and hands-free input
- Language learning and practice

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I change AI models?
Click the model name at the top of the chat and select a different one from the dropdown.

### Can I upload multiple files at once?
Yes, you can upload multiple files in a single conversation. Each file can be referenced separately.

### How long are chats saved?
All your chats are saved indefinitely until you delete them manually.

### Can I edit my messages after sending?
Yes, click the edit icon next to your message to modify it.

### What's the difference between models?
Different models have different strengths. Try asking the same question to multiple models to see which works best for your needs.

### Is there a message limit?
Free plan has daily limits. Pro plans offers unlimited messages. Check your plan details for specifics.

### Can I export my conversations?
Yes, use the export feature in chat history to save conversations as text or PDF.

### How do I delete a chat?
Open the chat history, find the conversation, and click the delete icon.
