# Quick Start — InstaAiTools

On this page you’ll learn how to begin using InstaAiTools and explore all the core features available inside the assistant.

## InstaAiTools Chat: Your AI Anywhere
On any website, you can open InstaAiTools instantly using your assigned keyboard shortcut or by clicking the InstaAiTools floating icon on the side of your browser.
Once opened, you can chat about **anything** — ask questions, generate ideas, analyze content, or work with files.

## Read: PDFs, Articles, Webpages & Images
Use the **Read** feature to let InstaAiTools process text from webpages, URLs, screenshots, images, or PDFs.
The assistant will extract key information, summarize the content, and allow deeper conversation with the material.

## Search: Get Instant Answers
Click the InstaAiTools icon and type your question into the search bar.
InstaAiTools searches the web, analyzes results, and provides a concise, structured answer — saving you time and improving accuracy.

## Write: Compose Anything Effortlessly
The **Write** tool provides two modes: **Compose** and **Reply**.
- **Compose**: Choose length, format, tone, and language. InstaAiTools generates polished writing tailored to your instructions.
- **Reply**: Paste a message, email, or comment and generate a smart, context-aware reply instantly.

## Translate: Break Language Barriers
InstaAiTools offers quick translation for webpages, selected text, uploaded files, and chat responses.
Translate content into 50+ languages while keeping clarity and natural flow.

## Image Generation: Create with AI
Describe any idea and InstaAiTools will generate an image based on your prompt.
You can refine style, composition, or upload reference images for improved accuracy.

## Quick Actions: AI Tools at Your Cursor
Highlight any text on a webpage and the **Quick Actions** toolbar appears beneath the selection.
Options include:
- Translate
- Summarize
- Explain
- Rewrite
- Improve tone
- Extend content

> _Tip:_ You can customize the order of quick actions inside InstaAiTools settings.

## Smart Compose: One-Click Writing
Whenever you’re inside a text field, look for the InstaAiTools writing icon.
Clicking it opens a quick writer where you can:
- Rewrite text
- Adjust tone
- Generate new content
- Fix grammar instantly

## Search Enhance: Smarter Web Searching
InstaAiTools enhances your search engine pages by displaying AI-generated summaries next to results.
You can also click suggested questions to explore deeper insights.

## Video Summary: Save Time Watching Videos
On supported YouTube pages, click the **Summarize** button to instantly generate:
- Key points
- Time-stamped insights
- Topic breakdowns

> _Tip:_ Click any timestamp to jump directly to that moment in the video.
