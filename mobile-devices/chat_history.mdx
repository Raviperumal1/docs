On mobile and small-screen devices, Nexva provides a **Chat History Drawer** to make chat navigation easy without occupying screen space.

The drawer opens **from bottom to top** and contains search, conversation history, and quick access controls.

---

## When the Drawer Appears

The mobile chat history drawer is shown when:

- The device screen is small (mobile or tablet)
- The sidebar is hidden due to limited screen space
- The user taps the **History (ðŸ•˜)** icon at the top of the screen

---

## How to Open the Chat History Drawer

1. Tap the **History** icon in the top bar
2. A drawer slides **up from the bottom**
3. The drawer overlays the current screen

---

## Drawer Contents

The chat history drawer contains the following sections:

---

### Search Conversations

At the top of the drawer, a search input is displayed.

**Search Features:**
- Search conversations by keyword
- Results update as you type
- Helps quickly locate older chats

---

### Chat History List

Below the search input, the drawer displays chat history.

#### When Chat History Is Available

- A list of previous conversations is shown
- Chats are ordered by most recent activity
- Tap a chat to open and continue the conversation
- The selected chat is highlighted

---

#### When No Chat History Is Available

- An empty state is shown
- Message example:
  > **No chats yet**
  > Start a new conversation to see it here.

No list is displayed if no conversations exist.

---

---

## Closing the Drawer

You can close the drawer by:

- Swiping the drawer down
- Tapping outside the drawer area
- Selecting a chat

---

## User Experience Behavior

- Drawer animation is smooth and responsive
- Drawer state resets when closed
- Search input is cleared when the drawer closes
- Only one drawer can be open at a time

---

## Best Practices

- Use search to find conversations quickly
- Delete unused chats to keep history clean
- Close the drawer before starting long conversations
- Use the drawer instead of sidebar on mobile devices

---

## Getting Started

Tap the **History** icon on mobile to open the chat history drawer and manage your conversations efficiently.
