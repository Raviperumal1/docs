At Nexva, protecting your data is a top priority.
This page explains how we collect, process, store, and protect user data, as well as your rights and responsibilities when using the Nexva platform.

This document is intended to provide transparency and clarity about Nexva’s security and privacy practices.

---

## Our Commitment to Data Protection

Nexva is built with security and privacy at its core.
We follow industry best practices to ensure that user data is handled responsibly, securely, and in compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

Our goals are to:
- Protect user confidentiality
- Prevent unauthorized access
- Minimize data exposure
- Maintain trust and transparency

---

## What Data We Collect

Nexva collects only the data necessary to provide and improve our services.

### Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:
- Email address
- Name or username
- Authentication credentials (securely stored)
- Subscription or plan details

---

### Usage Data

To operate and improve the platform, we may collect:
- Feature usage information
- Interaction logs
- Performance metrics
- Error and diagnostic data

This data helps us maintain service quality and reliability.

---

### Content You Provide

When using Nexva, you may provide:
- Chat prompts and messages
- Uploaded files (documents, images, data)
- Generated outputs (text or images)

This content is processed only to fulfill your requests.

---

## How Your Data Is Used

We use collected data to:
- Provide core platform functionality
- Process AI requests and responses
- Improve performance and reliability
- Detect and prevent abuse or fraud
- Respond to support requests
- Comply with legal obligations

We do **not** sell user data to third parties.

---

## AI Processing & Data Handling

### Prompt and Content Processing

- Prompts and uploaded content are processed to generate responses
- Data is handled in accordance with provider and platform policies
- Processing is limited to the scope of your request

---

### Model Providers

Nexva integrates with multiple AI model providers.
Data sent to these models is used **only** to generate the requested output and is subject to the provider’s data handling policies.

We carefully evaluate providers to meet security and compliance standards.

---

## Data Storage & Retention

### Storage

- Data is stored using secure infrastructure
- Access is restricted to authorized systems and personnel
- Encryption is used where applicable

---

### Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to:
- Provide services
- Meet legal or regulatory requirements
- Resolve disputes or enforce agreements

Users may request data deletion in accordance with applicable laws.

---

## Security Measures

Nexva implements multiple layers of security, including:

- Secure authentication mechanisms
- Access control and authorization
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Regular security monitoring
- Infrastructure-level protections

Security practices are reviewed and updated continuously.

---

## Access Control

- Only authorized personnel can access sensitive systems
- Access is limited based on role and necessity
- Internal access is logged and monitored

---

## Third-Party Services

Nexva may use trusted third-party services for:
- Payment processing
- Infrastructure hosting
- Analytics and monitoring

These providers are selected carefully and are required to meet security and privacy standards.

---

## Payment Security

- Payment information is processed through secure, compliant payment providers
- Nexva does not store full credit card details
- Transactions are protected using industry-standard security practices

---

## User Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request data correction
- Request data deletion
- Object to certain data processing activities
- Request data portability

Requests can be submitted through Nexva support.

---

## Cookies & Tracking

Nexva may use cookies or similar technologies to:
- Maintain user sessions
- Improve performance
- Understand usage patterns

You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings.

---

## Children’s Privacy

Nexva is not intended for use by children under the age required by applicable law.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

---

## Incident Response & Breach Handling

In the event of a security incident:
- We investigate promptly
- We take immediate steps to contain and mitigate impact
- We notify affected users when required by law

Our incident response procedures are designed to minimize risk and disruption.

---

## Data Deletion & Account Closure

Users may request:
- Account deletion
- Removal of stored personal data

Requests are processed according to legal, regulatory, and operational requirements.

---

## Compliance & Regulations

Nexva aims to align with applicable data protection and privacy regulations, including:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- Other regional data protection laws, where applicable

Compliance efforts are ongoing and regularly reviewed.

## Transparency & Updates

- Platform changes

## Your Responsibilities as a User

To help keep your account secure:
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Do not share login credentials
- Log out from shared or public devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately
