# What is Nexva?

Nexva is an all-in-one AI assistant platform that combines multiple powerful AI models and tools in a single interface. Instead of paying for and managing multiple AI subscriptions, Nexva gives you everything you need in one place.

## What Does Nexva Support?

Nexva provides access to the following AI capabilities:

### 1. AI Chat with Multiple Models

Chat with the world's best AI language models:

- **GPT-4o & GPT-4o-mini** (OpenAI)
- **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** (Anthropic)
- **Google Gemini Pro**
- **DeepSeek V3**
- And more open-source models

**What You Can Do:**
- Have conversations and get answers to questions
- Generate content (articles, emails, posts)
- Get coding help and debugging assistance
- Translate text between languages
- Get creative writing assistance
- Research and summarize information

---

### 2. AI Image Generation

Create images from text descriptions or reference images.

**What You Can Do:**
- **Text-to-Image**: Describe what you want and AI generates it
  - Example: "A sunset over mountains, photorealistic, 4K quality"
- **Image-to-Image**: Upload a reference image to guide the generation
  - Use existing images as style references
  - Transform and modify uploaded images

**Supported Models:**
- DALL-E 3
- Stable Diffusion
- Midjourney-style models

**Use Cases:**
- Create marketing graphics
- Generate social media content
- Design logos and branding
- Create artwork and illustrations

---

### 3. AI Video Generation

Generate videos from text prompts or animate static images.

**What You Can Do:**
- **Text-to-Video**: Describe a scene and AI creates video
  - Example: "Ocean waves at sunset, cinematic quality"
- **Image-to-Video**: Upload an image and AI animates it
  - Bring photos to life with movement
  - Add motion to static designs

**Video Features:**
- Multiple aspect ratios (16:9, 9:16, 1:1)
- HD and 4K quality output
- 5-20 second video clips
- Camera controls (pan, zoom, tilt)

**Use Cases:**
- Create social media videos
- Generate marketing content
- Animate product photos
- Create video backgrounds

### 4. Chat with PDF

Upload documents and have conversations with them.

**What You Can Do:**
- Upload PDF, DOCX, PPTX, or TXT files
- Ask questions about the document content
- Get summaries of sections or entire documents
- Extract specific information (tables, data, quotes)
- Translate document sections
- Compare multiple documents

**Supported File Types:**
- PDF (including scanned documents with OCR)
- Microsoft Word (.docx)
- PowerPoint (.pptx)
- Text files (.txt)

**Use Cases:**
- Analyze research papers
- Review contracts and legal documents
- Study textbooks and course materials
- Extract data from reports
- Summarize meeting minutes

### 5. AI Math Solver

Solve mathematical problems with step-by-step explanations.

**What You Can Do:**
- Solve math problems from basic to advanced levels
- Get step-by-step solution explanations
- See visual graphs and diagrams
- Upload photos of handwritten problems

**Supported Math Topics:**
- Basic arithmetic and algebra
- Geometry and trigonometry
- Calculus (derivatives, integrals)
- Statistics and probability
- Linear algebra and matrices

**Input Methods:**
- Type equations in plain text
- Use LaTeX mathematical notation
- Upload images of math problems

**Use Cases:**
- Homework help
- Test preparation
- Learn mathematical concepts
- Verify solutions

---

### 6. AI Content Detection

Detect whether text was written by AI or by a human.

**What You Can Do:**
- Paste any text to analyze
- Get AI probability score (0-100%)
- See sentence-by-sentence breakdown
- Identify AI-specific writing patterns

**Detection Results:**
- AI probability percentage
- Human probability percentage
- Confidence level
- Highlighted AI indicators

**Use Cases:**
- Verify student assignments
- Check content authenticity
- Review outsourced content
- Ensure human-written quality

---

### 7. AI Humanizer

Convert AI-generated text into natural, human-like writing.

**What You Can Do:**
- Paste AI-written content
- Transform it to sound more natural and human
- Remove robotic or formulaic language
- Make content pass AI detection tools

**Humanization Levels:**
- **Light**: Minimal changes, keeps formal tone
- **Medium**: Balanced natural writing
- **Heavy**: Highly conversational and casual

**What Gets Changed:**
- Removes AI-specific phrases
- Varies sentence structure
- Adds personality and naturalness
- Makes writing more engaging

**Use Cases:**
- Improve AI-generated blog posts
- Make content more relatable
- Pass AI detection when needed
- Create authentic-sounding content
